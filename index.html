About Casa Real Shrine

The Casa Real Shrine, located in Malolos City, Bulacan, is a historical building that stands as a testament to the province’s rich colonial heritage. Originally constructed in 1580, it served as the office of the Spanish gobernadorcillo during the Spanish period.

Today, Casa Real is recognized as a national shrine and museum, showcasing relics, documents, and artifacts from the Spanish and revolutionary eras. It also highlights the role of Malolos as the birthplace of the First Philippine Republic.

The building has undergone several restorations and remains one of the most visited heritage sites in Bulacan.
